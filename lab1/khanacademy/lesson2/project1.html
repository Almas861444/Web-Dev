  • Han Tengri

    • Han Tengri is one of the highest mountains in Central Asia. It is located on the border of Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, and China. The mountain has a beautiful pyramid shape. Its peak is covered with marble. Many climbers want to reach its summit.

  • Alatau Mountains

    • The Alatau Mountains are located in Central Asia. They stretch across several countries, including Kazakhstan. The mountains have high peaks and glaciers. Many rivers start in the Alatau Mountains. They are popular for tourism and hiking.
